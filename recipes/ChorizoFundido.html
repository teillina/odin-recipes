Chorizo!

Description

Very Yummy Food!

Ingredients

  • Bell Pepper
  • Cream Cheese
  • Chile Pepper

Steps

  1. Place chorizo, green onions, red bell pepper, poblano chilies, and jalapeno in a cold skillet. Turn heat to medium-high. As skillet comes up to temperature break up chorizo into very small pieces. Continue cooking until sausage is browned and peppers are barely cooked through, 4 to 6 minutes. Remove from heat. If sausage releases a lot of grease, mop up some with a paper towel.
  2. Mix cream cheese, sausage mixture, Monterey Jack cheese, Cheddar cheese, and sour cream in a bowl. Transfer to a shallow, heat-proof baking dish.
  3. Set oven rack about 6 inches from the heat source and preheat the oven's broiler.
  4. Broil on high until browned and bubbly, 5 or 6 minutes.
